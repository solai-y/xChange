Yonsei University

About Us

Yonsei University, often referred to simply as "Yonsei," is a leading private research university located in Seoul, South Korea. It is part of the prestigious SKY universities in South Korea, along with Seoul National University and Korea University. Yonsei is known for its strong academic programs, rigorous research, and vibrant campus life.
